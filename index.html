Michael Mattioli
Michael Mattioli lecturing in Düsseldorf, Germany (2017)

Michael Mattioli is a Professor of Law at the Indiana University Maurer School of Law in Bloomington. His research focuses on technology, public policy, and ethnography. Mattioli's scholarship and commentary has been published in leading academic journals, books, and periodicals. He co-edited Big Data is Not a Monolith, published by the MIT Press in 2016. In 2018, Indiana University honored Mattioli with a Trustees Teaching Award.

A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Law School and Tufts University, Mattioli has held fellowship appointments at Berkeley Law and Michigan Law - both funded by Microsoft Corporation. Before attending law school, he worked as a microchip designer.
